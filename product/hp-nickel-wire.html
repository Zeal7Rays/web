Product Overview

High Purity Nickel Wire

High Purity Nickel Wire is manufactured using advanced wire drawing and cold working technologies to achieve exceptional purity, dimensional accuracy and mechanical performance.

Our company supplies ultra-fine nickel wire with Ni purity up to 99.9975%, verified by independent laboratory analysis.

The wire is supplied on cylindrical coils and is suitable for demanding industrial, scientific and electronic applications.
