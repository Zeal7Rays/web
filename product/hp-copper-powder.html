Product Overview

High Purity Copper Powder

High Purity Ultrafine Copper Powder

Copper Powder is an electrolytic copper material produced through advanced precipitation technologies.

The powder features extremely high copper purity, uniform particle distribution and excellent stability, making it suitable for demanding industrial applications.

Ultra-fine particle sizes and ultra-high purity grades are available according to customer requirements.

Key Features

99.999%+ Purity

Ultra-high purity copper for advanced technologies.

Ultra-Fine Particles

Available in ultra-fine particle distributions.

Spherical Morphology

Excellent flowability and process stability.

Argon Stabilization

Available for highly sensitive applications.
