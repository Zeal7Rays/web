Tungsten Rods & Ingots

High-Purity Tungsten Rods and Ingots for Aerospace, Electronics, High Temperature Furnaces and Advanced Industrial Applications.

Product Overview

Tungsten Rods

Tungsten Ingots

Tungsten Rods and Ingots are refractory metal products manufactured through powder metallurgy, forging and drawing processes.

Tungsten provides high density, excellent heat resistance, corrosion resistance and high strength.

Products are available in various grades including VA, VA-1, VL, VT, VRN and SVI-1, with dimensions manufactured according to customer requirements.

Key Features

Ultra-High Density

One of the highest density engineering metals available.

Extreme Heat Resistance

Outstanding performance under high-temperature conditions.

Corrosion Resistance

Excellent stability in harsh operating environments.

Mechanical Strength

Exceptional hardness and structural durability.
