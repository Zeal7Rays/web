Product Overview

Tungsten Powder

Particle Morphology (SEM)

Tungsten Powder is a high-purity refractory metal powder featuring exceptional density, high melting point, excellent corrosion resistance and superior mechanical performance.

Manufactured through the reduction of tungsten oxide or ammonium paratungstate (APT), Tungsten Powder offers uniform particle size distribution and excellent flowability for demanding industrial applications.

Typical Specifications

CAS Number 7440-33-7
Purity 99.0 % - 99.999 %
Particle Size (F.S.S.S.) 1.8 - 2.7 μm size up to 40.0 μm (Custom Available)
Oxygen Content 0.03 - 0.08 % depending on particle size
Apparent Density 2.5 - 2.7 g/cm³ depending on particle size
Melting Point 3,400 °C
Appearance Dark Gray Metallic Powder

Detailed specifications and particle size distributions can be provided upon request.
